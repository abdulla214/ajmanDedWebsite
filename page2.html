                                 Chairman's message
On behalf of the Department of Economic Development -Ajman, I would like to welcome you to the official website of the Department of Control and Consumer Protection launched in the hope of strengthening our positive communication with you. We hope that this platform will provide you with useful and reliable information about consumer protection laws in line with our efforts to enhance investors’ confidence in the local market and accelerate the growth of the national economy.
Ajman and the UAE have along prioritized consumer protection as one of the key pillars for the creation of a fair and balanced consumer-focused and investment-friendly environment. As part of our mandate, rest assured that DED Ajman will continue to protect consumer rights to bring happiness to the people. This is aligned with the directives of H.H. Sheikh Humaid bin Rashid Al Nuaimi, Supreme Council Member and Ruler of Ajman, who said: ‘Happiness is the path towards creativity and progress.’
Through this online platform, we invite you to share your suggestions, observations, opinions, and other issues you wish to bring to our attention. We commit to respond quickly to your needs and deliver the highest quality of service according to the goal of Ajman Vision 2021 to create a ‘happy society that contributes to building a green economy that lives up to the spirit of the Union.’
Thank you.

His Highness Sheikh Ahmed Bin Humaid Al-Nuaimi

Chairmen of Department of Economic Development

                                 Director's Message
We are pleased to launch this website, which represents a strong boost towards the efforts to establish a strong relationship and effective partnership with consumers to combat commercial fraud and reduce illegal practices. This quality-focused step is part of our continuing move to make consumer protection a part of a well-established culture within the local community and one of the cornerstones of social well-being and economic development, which falls in line with Ajman Vision 2021.
We are confident that the website will serve as a direct and interactive platform, which will raise awareness about consumer behavior and effective consumer protection—acting as a foundation for enhancing market stability and stimulating economical and investment activity that supports all sectors in the ambitious roadmap to development. We reaffirm our commitment to work in accordance with set standards and guidelines aimed at protecting and promoting the welfare of consumers; raise its confidence and enhance competitiveness--reinforcing Ajman’s position as a highly attractive destination for investment locally and regionally.
In conclusion, we hope that the website will be an advanced step in protecting consumer’s rights, which is a key priority for DED Ajman and represents efforts to develop a diversified competitive economy for sustainable development in the emirate.

His Excellency (H.E) Ali Eissa Al Nuaimi

Director General, Department of Economic Development - Ajman
